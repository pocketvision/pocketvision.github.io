Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

PocketVision respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. No Cloud Upload of Photos or Videos

PocketVision performs AI processing locally on your device. Your photos and videos are not uploaded to our servers.

2. Photo Library Access

The app may request access to your photo library solely for selecting and saving media files that you explicitly choose.

3. No Account Required

PocketVision does not require user registration or login.

4. No Tracking or Analytics

PocketVision does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics services, or tracking technologies.

5. Local Processing

All AI inference and media processing are performed on-device whenever possible.

6. Data Security

Since your media remains on your device, you maintain control over your personal content.

7. Children's Privacy

PocketVision is not intended for children under the age of 13.
